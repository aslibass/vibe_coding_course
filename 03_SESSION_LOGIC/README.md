## 🗄️ Core Concept: The Filing Cabinet

When developers talk about the "Backend" or a "Database", they are talking about a permanent storage space. We like to call it the Filing Cabinet.

- **The Frontend (What you see):** This is the beautiful desk in the lobby. Your React code creates the desk, the buttons, and the forms. 
- **The Backend (The Filing Cabinet):** This is the secure room in the back office. When someone fills out a form on the desk, a digital runner takes that paper and files it away securely. When you open the app later, the runner fetches the paper and puts it back on the desk.

Today, we are going to build an app that actually talks to a Filing Cabinet. We will use a tool called **Firebase**. Firebase is Google's incredibly powerful, free, invisible Filing Cabinet.

It lives in the cloud, meaning your data is safe even if your computer breaks.
